_

Our Problem

The UC Alert system relies on email notications to warn students about emergencies or inclement weather happening on the UC Campus. However, this system only features a few details. Moreover, some students could have a hard time identifying these addresses.

Our Solution

Alert Map is a web application that reveals to users the incidents reported in real time. Allowing users to identify exactly where incidents are reported in. In addition, Alert Maps will suggest to users an alternater route that is safer to reach their destination. Furthermore, the data collected is based on the UC public safety alert system.
